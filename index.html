About Me

I am a native Jamaican who migrated with my family to America to gain a better education and life. Growing up in New York played a major part on my outlook of where I wanted to be. My fortunes of being in America was not living in New York City. It was a culture shock and took a while as a young kid to adjust but learned a lot with the challenges of city life.

Coming from the Islands, fish was my favorite food along with many other fruits and vegetables. in America I discovered pizza and fast foods which changed my pallet for the things I cherished but was less available. I loved pizza!
